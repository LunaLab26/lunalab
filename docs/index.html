LunaVault FuseBox

Preserve every moment. Losslessly.

Turn a day's worth of camera clips into one properly archived master file — with both microphones kept intact and in sync — plus a quick tool for trimming and colour-grading clips to share.

Download View on GitHub

What it does

Lossless merge

Clips are stream-copied — no re-encode, no quality loss. Mismatched clips are conformed at high quality.

Two-mic in sync

Bluetooth mic and on-board WAV kept as separate tracks, aligned with GCC-PHAT and drift correction.

Slow-motion aware

Detects slow-mo clips and time-stretches the WAV (pitch-corrected) to match the slowed video.

Colour-grade & share

Trim a moment, apply one of 28 film/cinema LUTs, and export a share-ready MP4.

Pre-flight & live ETA

See exactly what the merge will do before you commit, with live MB/s and ETA during the render.

Light / dark / system

A clean, grouped interface that follows your system theme. Windows and Steam Deck ready.

The two-mic idea

When you record with a wireless mic, the camera saves that mic into the MP4 while a separate WAV preserves the camera's on-board mic. FuseBox keeps both — clarity and a safety net — in one file.

Camera track (MP4)

Wireless Bluetooth mic — close, clear voice. Kept stream-copied, bit-exact.

WAV backup

On-board camera mic — fuller room sound and a safety net. Stored lossless (ALAC).
